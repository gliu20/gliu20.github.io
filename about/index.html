A Little About Me...

I was born in Toronto, Canada, but like many others, my family and I moved to the United States in pursuit of the American Dream.

Originally, we moved to Queens, NY. There, I learned many invaluable life lessons. I learned to be humble and to be grateful. Living among those who were less fortunate revealed to me how much we take most things for granted.

In 2010, my family and I moved to Westchester, NY, and settled down in Scarsdale. Although I had left Queens, my experience there would never leave me. The stark contrast between the wealthy and the less fortunate shocked me—it was something that I could never forget. I had always wanted to help do something about it, but I never had the ability to. It was only until after discovering programming, did I have the ability to help shape the world.

My middle name, Zhen Yu, means shake the world. I had always wondered how I would shake the world, but after discovering programming, I knew the path I would take. For me, coding is like a superpower. It is the means through which anyone can help change the world, and I wanted to live up to my name and do just that.
